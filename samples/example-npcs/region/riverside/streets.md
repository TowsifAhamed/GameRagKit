# Streets of Riverside

## Upper District

The upper district surrounds the keep and houses nobles, wealthy merchants, and key government buildings.

### North Gate

The North Gate is one of four main entrances to the upper district. It is:
- Heavily guarded at all times
- The primary entrance for diplomatic visitors
- Connected to the main trade road leading north
- Monitored by Jake, a stoic and duty-first guard known for his careful scrutiny

## Trade Routes

During the pre-siege era, the streets bustle with:
- Merchants selling exotic goods
- Craftsmen displaying their wares
- Town criers announcing the latest royal decrees
- Guards patrolling regularly

## Notable Locations

### The Brass Token Inn

Located near the North Gate, this inn is known for serving royal messengers. Those bearing brass tokens - official credentials from the king - are given free lodging and meals here.

### Market Square

The heart of Riverside commerce, where goods from across the kingdom are traded daily.
